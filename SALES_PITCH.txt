6. WHEN THEY ASK ABOUT SECURITY
----------------------------------------------------------------

Security was a top priority when building this:

- Passwords hashed — we never store plain passwords
- Role-based access — admin, dentist, receptionist, and patient
  each see only what they are supposed to see
- Email verification — every new account must verify their email
  before logging in
- Encrypted data — sensitive information encrypted at rest
  using AES-256
- Rate limiting — login attempts blocked after repeated failures
  to prevent brute force attacks
- Audit logging — every action is logged (who did what and when)
- HTTPS only — all data transmitted securely
- RA 10173 compliant — built following the Philippine Data
  Privacy Act

Patient data stays protected. You stay compliant.
